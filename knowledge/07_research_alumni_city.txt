[Research at UNNC]
UNNC hosts active research across all three faculties, mirroring the research excellence of the UK campus.
Research Centres & Institutes:
  - International Doctoral Innovation Centre (IDIC): Collaborative PhD programs with industry partners.
  - UNNC Academy of Advanced Interdisciplinary Studies: Cross-disciplinary research.
  - Centre for Sustainable Energy Technologies (CSET): Renewable energy, green buildings, energy efficiency.
  - Ningbo NFTZ Research Academy: Research collaboration with Ningbo Free Trade Zone.
  - Asia Research Institute: Regional studies, Asian economics, politics.
  - Centre for Geospatial Science: GIS, spatial data, remote sensing.
Research Opportunities for Undergraduates:
  - Summer Research Internships: Work with faculty on research projects. Some carry stipends.
  - Final Year Projects (Dissertations): A significant research component in most programs.
  - Undergraduate Research Opportunities Program (UROP): Available in some departments.
  - Students can approach lecturers to join ongoing research projects.
PhD Programs:
  - Available in all departments.
  - Duration: 3-4 years (full-time).
  - Funding: UNNC PhD scholarships cover tuition + living stipend for top candidates.
  - Joint supervision with UK campus is common.
  - Application: Contact potential supervisors first, then apply through the UNNC graduate admissions portal.

[Graduation & Alumni]
Graduation Ceremonies: Held in July at the Sir Jesse Boot Foundation Building. Two ceremonies per day (morning and afternoon).
  - Dress code: Academic gown and mortarboard (hired through an approved supplier, info sent by email).
  - Guests: Each graduate may bring 2-3 guests (tickets allocated).
  - Photography: Professional photographer available on the day.
Degree Certificate:
  - Awarded by the University of Nottingham (UK). The certificate does not distinguish between campuses.
  - Also receive a Chinese Ministry of Education certified degree recognition (学位认证).
  - Electronic degree verification is available through the UK HEDD service.
Alumni Network:
  - Join the UNNC Alumni Association (automatic upon graduation).
  - Access to the University of Nottingham global alumni network (300,000+ worldwide).
  - Alumni events, networking, mentoring, career support.
  - Alumni LinkedIn group and WeChat groups available.
  - Alumni can continue to use the university email and library e-resources for 1 year after graduation.
Transcripts & Certificates after Graduation:
  - Order through the University of Nottingham online store or contact The Hub.

[Ningbo City Guide for UNNC Students]
Ningbo (宁波) is a major port city in Zhejiang Province with a population of ~9 million.
Climate: Subtropical. Hot, humid summers (June-September, 30-38°C). Cool to cold winters (December-February, 2-10°C). Rainy season (plum rain) in June-July.
Key Areas:
  - Tianyi Square (天一广场): Main commercial centre. Shopping, dining, entertainment. ~40 min from campus by bus.
  - Yinzhou Wanda (银洲万达): Nearest large mall. Shopping, cinema, restaurants. ~15 min by bus.
  - Old Bund (老外滩): Historic waterfront area. Bars, restaurants, European architecture. Good for weekend outings.
  - South Ningbo Commercial Area (南部商务区): Modern business district near campus. Restaurants and coffee shops.
  - East Qianhu Lake (东钱湖): Beautiful lake area for hiking, cycling, picnics. ~30 min from campus.
Day Trips from Ningbo:
  - Hangzhou: ~1.5h by high-speed rail. West Lake, tea plantations, cultural sites.
  - Shanghai: ~2h by high-speed rail. International metropolis.
  - Zhoushan (Putuo Mountain): ~2h by bus/ferry. Famous Buddhist island.
  - Xikou (溪口): Chiang Kai-shek's hometown. ~1h by bus. Historic town.
Local Specialties (Food): Crab (particularly hairy crab in autumn), seafood (鱼鲞, 大黄鱼), Ningbo tangyuan (rice dumplings), Fengtou (年糕).
Language: Standard Mandarin is widely spoken. Local Ningbo dialect (宁波话) may be heard but is not needed.
