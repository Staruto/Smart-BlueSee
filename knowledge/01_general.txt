[Campus Overview]
University Name: University of Nottingham Ningbo China (UNNC)
Founded: 2004, the first Sino-foreign cooperative university in China.
Parent University: University of Nottingham (UK, founded 1881, Russell Group).
Location: 199 Taikang East Road, Yinzhou District, Ningbo, Zhejiang Province, China (315100).
Campus Size: Approximately 500 mu (33 hectares). The campus features modern architecture combining British and Chinese design elements.
Students: More than 10,000 students from over 70 countries and regions.
Teaching Language: English (all degree programs are taught in English).
Degrees: All degrees are awarded by the University of Nottingham (UK), internationally recognized.
Ranking: The University of Nottingham is ranked #97 in the QS World University Rankings 2026.
Accreditations: Russell Group member (UK), U21 network, QAA-reviewed, EQUIS/AMBA/AACSB (Business School triple accreditation).
Motto: "A city is not built in a day." (Campus motto displayed on the Sir Jesse Boot Foundation Building).
UNNC is part of the University of Nottingham's global network, which includes campuses in the UK (Nottingham), China (Ningbo), and Malaysia (Semenyih).
Employability: 9 out of 10 UNNC graduates enter global top universities or companies (2023/24 report).

[Emergency & Security]
24-hour Emergency Hotline: +86 (0)574 8818 0000 (handles all campus emergencies).
Campus Security Office: 24-hour patrol and monitoring. Tel: 8818 0110.
Campus Clinic: Building 23, Room 109. Tel: 8818 0120. Hours: Mon-Fri 08:30-17:00.
  - Provides basic medical care, first aid, and referrals to local hospitals.
  - For after-hours medical emergencies, call 8818 0000 (24h hotline).
Nearest Hospital: Ningbo Yinzhou No. 2 Hospital (~10 min by taxi from campus).
Fire Emergency: Call 119 (national fire emergency) or 8818 0000 (campus).
Police: Call 110 (national police) or contact Campus Security 8818 0110.
Ambulance: Call 120 (national ambulance service).
Important: In any campus emergency, call 8818 0000 first — the 24h operator can dispatch security, medical, or fire response immediately.
