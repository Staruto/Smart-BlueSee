  - BEng Civil Engineering — structural engineering, geotechnics, construction management.

  - BSc Mathematics with Applied Mathematics — pure and applied mathematics, statistics, mathematical modelling.
  - BEng Mechanical Engineering — thermodynamics, mechanics, design, manufacturing.
NEW Programs (starting September 2026):
  - BSc Big Data Management and Applications — NEW, first cohort 2026. Covers data analytics, data governance, and data-driven decision making.
Note: UNNC previously offered standalone BSc Computer Science. As of current listings, the undergraduate CS program is BSc Computer Science with Artificial Intelligence.

  - MSc Data Science — big data analytics, machine learning, data visualization.
  - MSc Civil Engineering
  - MSc Environmental Engineering
  - MSc Electrical and Electronic Engineering
  - Various PhD programs available in all departments.
Note: All FoSE programs are taught in English and lead to University of Nottingham (UK) degrees.
School Offices: Each department has a School Office for academic queries, module enrollment issues, and extenuating circumstances claims.

[Year 1 Foundation Program]
Many Chinese students enter UNNC through a Foundation Year (Year 0) or directly into Year 1.
Preliminary Year: A one-year foundation program for students who do not meet direct entry requirements. Covers English language, mathematics, and introductory subjects.
Direct Entry: Students with strong Gaokao scores or international qualifications (A-levels, IB) may enter Year 1 directly.
2+2 Program: Students can study Years 1-2 at UNNC and transfer to the UK campus (University of Nottingham) for Years 3-4.
4+0 Program: Complete all 4 years at UNNC campus.
All students receive the same University of Nottingham degree regardless of pathway.

[Grading System]
UNNC follows the UK grading system:
- First Class (1st): 70% and above — Outstanding performance.
- Upper Second (2:1): 60-69% — Very good performance. Most employers require 2:1 minimum.
- Lower Second (2:2): 50-59% — Good performance.
- Third Class (3rd): 40-49% — Satisfactory performance.
- Fail: Below 40%.
GPA is NOT used. The UK percentage system applies.
Module Assessment: Typically a combination of coursework (essays, lab reports, projects) and exams. Weightings vary by module — check Moodle for details.
Exam Boards: Results are confirmed by Exam Boards at the end of each semester. Provisional marks on Blue Castle may change after the Board.
Academic Integrity: Plagiarism and collusion are serious offenses. Turnitin is used for all submitted work. Penalties range from mark deduction to expulsion.
