Student support
Student Support
We offer a wide range of support to offer holders and incoming students. These services often include visas, insurance, pre-arrival advice, accommodation support, induction, and introductions to career advisory services and university life. A number of professional service units support incoming students including Global Engagement Office, Student Affairs Office, and Admissions & Career Development Office.
Alumni community
Graduation is just the start of a lifelong relationship with your University. We're a community of over 12000 former students now living around the world, who support each other and current students to fulfill our potential.
Extracurricular activities
There are over 100 student clubs and societies on campus which can enrich your extracurricular life here in China.
Career services
We are a world top 30 choice for employers according to the QS, and our graduates have found jobs with leading global companies such as Neilsen, AB InBev, Hilton, IKEA, KPMG, Nestle, ARUP, Standard Chartered Bank, DHL and Google.
