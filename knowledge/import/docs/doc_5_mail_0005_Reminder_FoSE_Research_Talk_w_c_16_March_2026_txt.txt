Subject: Reminder: FoSE Research Talk (w/c 16 March 2026) Source: Outlook Web Inbox Dear All, Hope the email finds you well. It is with great please that we invite you to take part in research talks in w/c 16 March 2026. You will find the detailed information below. Talk 1 Date & Time: 13:00-14:30, Monday 16 March 2026 Venue: IEB 122 Speaker: Professor Xin Fan, Dalian University of Technology Title: Optimization Meets Deep Medical Image Analysis Biography: Xin Fan’s main research interests include computer vision and health computing. He has led two Key Projects and three General/Young Scientist Projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation of China (NSFC), and has participated as a core member in one Major Program of the NSFC. He has published over 100 papers in leading journals and conferences in the field, with more than 12,000 citations on Google Scholar, and has received four Best Paper Awards / nominations at major international conferences. He has been honored with the First Prize of the Liaoning Provincial Natural Science Award and the First Prize of the Wu Wenjun Artificial Intelligence Natural Science Award. His research outcomes have been successfully deployed in national major mission-oriented applications and in the field of underwater robotics. He is also dedicated to the exploration and practice of cultivating internationalized software engineering talent, and has received one Second Prize of the National Teaching Achievement Award and two Liaoning Provincial Teaching Achievement Awards. Abstract: Optimization techniques play a crucial role in medical image analysis, especially in improving performance and generalization for image reconstruction, registration, and segmentation. This talk introduces a hierarchical optimization framework to enhance the efficiency and stability of reconstruction, registration, and segmentation, substantially improving model interpretability and generalization..
