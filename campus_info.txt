[Common Procedures]
1. Student ID Card: Lost cards must be reported to The Hub (Portland 120). Replacement fee: 30 RMB. Processing time: 3-5 working days.
2. Course Enrollment: Use 'NottinghamHub' (hub.nottingham.ac.uk) during the module enrollment window (typically 1-2 weeks before semester starts). Contact your School Office for module issues.
3. Wi-Fi Access:
   - SSID: eduroam
   - Username: username@nottingham.edu.cn (e.g., scxyy1@nottingham.edu.cn)
   - Password: Your university account password.
   - Troubleshooting: Clear saved eduroam profiles and reconnect. Contact IT Desk if issues persist.
4. Moodle: Main learning platform (moodle.nottingham.ac.uk). Login with university account. All course materials, assignments, and announcements are posted here.
5. Blue Castle: For checking exam results (bluecastle.nottingham.ac.uk). Results typically released 2-3 weeks after exam period.
6. Transcript Request: Apply through The Hub. Official transcripts: 20 RMB/copy. Processing: 5 working days.
7. Leave of Absence: Contact your Personal Tutor first, then apply through The Hub with supporting documentation.

[Campus Life Guide]
Canteens:
  - Canteen 1 (Teaching Area): Chinese food, noodles, rice dishes. Hours: 07:00-20:00.
  - Canteen 2 (Dorm Area): Halal options, diverse cuisines, bakery. Hours: 07:00-21:00.
  - Canteen 3 (North Area): Chinese food, hot pot. Hours: 11:00-13:00, 17:00-19:00.
  - Starbucks: In the Library Building & Portland Building. Hours: 08:00-21:00.
  - Various snack shops and convenience stores around the dormitory area.
Shuttle Bus: Campus Loop (free, every 15 min during term). Weekend Downtown Bus to Tianyi Square. Schedule available at The Hub reception.
Printing: Printers in Library, Computer Labs (Portland Building), and some faculty buildings. Use ID card to release print jobs. Cost: B&W 0.1 RMB/page, Color 0.5 RMB/page.
Sports Facilities: Gymnasium, swimming pool, tennis courts, basketball courts, football pitch. Gym membership: ~50 RMB/semester for students.
Student Clubs: 100+ registered clubs and societies. Join during Freshers' Fair (start of each semester) or contact the Student Union.
